A trip to Mount Hermon

Place description:

This is a beautiful and snowy mountain with a particularly spectacular view.
It also has various and varied attractions that give you a unique extreme experience.

Types of attractions on site:

  1. funicular
  2. Extreme slide
  3. Ice Skating

Required equipment:

  • Warm and thick clothes
  • comfortable shoes
  • Warm coat
  • A hat that also covers the ears
  • Gloves
